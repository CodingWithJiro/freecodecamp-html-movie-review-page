Movie Review: Zootopia

"Zootopia" is a 2016 Disney animated film set in a world where anthropomorphic animals live in a city called Zootopia, where predator and prey species coexist. The story follows Judy Hopps, a rookie bunny cop, who teams up with con artist fox Nick Wilde to uncover a mystery involving missing predators

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (9.2/10)

Cast Members

  • Jason Bateman as Nick Wilde
  • Idris Elba as Chief Bogo
  • Jenny Slate as Bellwether
